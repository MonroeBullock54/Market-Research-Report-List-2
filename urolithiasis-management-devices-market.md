<p><p>The urolithiasis management devices market encompasses several key types: lithotripters, stone removal devices, and ureteral stents. Lithotripters use shock waves to break down stones into smaller fragments, facilitating their passage. Stone removal devices, such as forceps and baskets, assist in the physical extraction of stones during surgical procedures. Ureteral stents are flexible tubes placed in the ureter to alleviate blockage and ensure urine flow, promoting healing and comfort in patients with urinary stones.</p></p>
