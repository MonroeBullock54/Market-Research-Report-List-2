<p><ul><li>ROMP Process</li><li>mCOC Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>COC (Cyclic Olefin Copolymer) resin market features two significant processing types: the ROMP (Ring Opening Metathesis Polymerization) and mCOC (Modified Cyclic Olefin Copolymer) processes. The ROMP process allows for precise control over polymer architecture and molecular weight, enhancing performance characteristics. In contrast, the mCOC process modifies standard COC resins to improve thermal stability and chemical resistance. Both processes cater to diverse applications in packaging, medical devices, and optics, driven by the demand for high-performance lightweight materials.</p></p>
