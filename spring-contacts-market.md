<p><p>Spring contacts can be classified into two market types: unidirectional and multidirectional. Unidirectional spring contacts allow electrical connections to flow in one direction, providing reliability in applications where only one-way current is needed. In contrast, multidirectional spring contacts enable connections in multiple directions, facilitating flexible designs and improved connectivity. This versatility in multidirectional contacts makes them ideal for applications requiring dynamic movement or change, such as automotive or consumer electronics, where space and orientation can vary.</p></p>
