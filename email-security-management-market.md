<p><p>Email security management encompasses two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise solutions involve installing security software directly on local servers, providing organizations with greater control over data and compliance, but requiring significant maintenance and resources. In contrast, cloud-based email security services are hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, ease of deployment, and automatic updates. This model allows organizations to focus on core activities while benefiting from enhanced security protocols and reduced administrative burdens.</p></p>
