<p><p>The Blood Urea Nitrogen (BUN) diagnostics market includes various assay types such as ELISA, colorimetric, and spectrophotometric methods. ELISA utilizes enzyme-linked antibodies for sensitive detection of urea levels. Colorimetric assays rely on color change reactions to quantify urea concentration, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Spectrophotometric methods measure the absorption of light at specific wavelengths to determine urea levels, providing high accuracy and precision. Each method caters to different lab requirements and patient needs within the diagnostic landscape.</p></p>
