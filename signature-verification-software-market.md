<p><ul><li>Static Signature Verification</li><li>Dynamic Signature Verification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Signature verification software is divided into two main types: static and dynamic verification. Static signature verification analyzes scanned images of signatures, focusing on the overall shape and features, making it suitable for applications such as document authentication. In contrast, dynamic signature verification assesses the signature's biometric data during the act of signing, including speed, pressure, and stroke patterns. This real-time analysis offers enhanced security for transactions like electronic banking and identity verification, ensuring greater authenticity and fraud protection.</p></p>
