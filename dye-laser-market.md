<p><p>The dye laser market is primarily categorized into two types based on the dye medium used: organic compound liquids and inorganic compound liquids. In contrast, inorganic compound liquids encompass metal-based dyes, providing stable output and longer operational lifespans. Both types are essential in fields such as telecommunications, laser displays, and scientific research, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
