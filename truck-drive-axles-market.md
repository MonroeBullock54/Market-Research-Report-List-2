<p><p>The truck drive axles market consists of three primary types: single, tandem, and tridem axles. Single axles are designed for lighter loads and offer better maneuverability. Tandem axles, which feature two sets of wheels, provide increased load capacity and stability, making them ideal for heavier trucks. Tridem axles, with three sets of wheels, are used for maximum load-bearing capacity, often in specialized applications like heavy hauling. Each type caters to distinct operational needs and vehicle configurations.</p></p>
