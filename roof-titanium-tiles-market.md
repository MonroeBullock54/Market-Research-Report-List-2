<p><p>The Roof Titanium Tiles market includes various types of underlayment materials essential for roofing systems. Asphalt-saturated felt, or felt paper, provides a reliable moisture barrier but is heavier and less durable. Rubberized asphalt features a self-adhesive layer, enhancing waterproofing and flexibility, making it ideal for extreme weather. Non-bitumen synthetics, or synthetic felt paper, are lightweight and resistant to tearing, offering superior longevity and performance. Each material serves specific needs in roofing, balancing cost, durability, and installation ease.</p></p>
