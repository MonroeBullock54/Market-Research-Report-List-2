<p><strong>The Automotive Sledge Testing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Decelerator Sled Testing System</li><li>Catapult Sled Testing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Sledge Testing System market consists of two primary types: Decelerator Sled Testing Systems and Catapult Sled Testing Systems. Decelerator sleds simulate vehicle deceleration during crash scenarios, allowing for the assessment of safety features and occupant protection by measuring impact forces. In contrast, catapult sleds launch test dummies or vehicles at controlled speeds to replicate collision dynamics, enabling analysis of vehicle performance and safety measures. Both systems are crucial for automotive research, development, and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
