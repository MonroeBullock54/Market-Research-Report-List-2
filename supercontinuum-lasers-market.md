<p><strong>The Supercontinuum Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MIR Laser</li><li>Visible/NIR Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Supercontinuum lasers are classified into two main types based on their wavelength output: Mid-Infrared (MIR) lasers and Visible/Near-Infrared (Visible/NIR) lasers. MIR lasers typically emit wavelengths ranging from 3 to 12 micrometers, making them ideal for applications in chemical sensing and environmental monitoring. In contrast, Visible/NIR lasers cover wavelengths from 400 to 900 nanometers, suited for diverse applications like biomedical imaging, spectroscopy, and material processing. Both types leverage nonlinear optical phenomena to generate a broad spectrum of wavelengths from a single source.</p></p>
