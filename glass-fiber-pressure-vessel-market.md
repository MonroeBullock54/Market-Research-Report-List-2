<p><ul><li>PAN-Based</li><li>Pitch-Based</li><li>Viscose-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass fiber pressure vessel market is segmented into three main types based on the precursor materials used: PAN-based, pitch-based, and viscose-based fibers. PAN-based vessels are known for their high strength and durability, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Pitch-based vessels offer excellent thermal and chemical resistance, ideal for demanding environments. Viscose-based fibers provide good tensile strength and are often used in more cost-effective solutions. Each type serves specific applications across industries, impacting performance and material selection.</p></p>
