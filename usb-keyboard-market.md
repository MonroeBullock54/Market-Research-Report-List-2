<p><p>The USB keyboard market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical and membrane keyboards. Mechanical keyboards utilize individual mechanical switches for each key, offering tactile feedback, durability, and customized user experiences, making them popular among gamers and typists. In contrast, membrane keyboards feature a softer, quieter design with a single pressure pad beneath the keys, providing a more compact and cost-effective solution. Each type serves distinct user preferences, with mechanical keyboards favored for performance and membrane keyboards for everyday use.</p></p>
