<p><p>The HVAC globe valve market is primarily categorized into three segments: three-port valves, two-port valves, and other types. Three-port valves facilitate mixing or diverting flow between two sources, making them ideal for temperature control in HVAC systems. Two-port valves, on the other hand, are simpler and regulate flow through one inlet and one outlet, commonly used for on/off control. Other market types encompass specialized valves designed for specific applications or environments, addressing unique system requirements.</p></p>
