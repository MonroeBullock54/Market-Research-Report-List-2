<p><p>The Cross-Linked Polyethylene (XLPE) insulated cable market is categorized primarily into 1 Core and 3 Core types. 1 Core cables consist of a single conductor, suitable for straightforward power distribution applications. In contrast, 3 Core cables contain three conductors within a single sheath, making them ideal for three-phase power systems, widely used in industrial and commercial settings. Both types offer excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and durability, making them essential for modern electrical infrastructure.</p></p>
