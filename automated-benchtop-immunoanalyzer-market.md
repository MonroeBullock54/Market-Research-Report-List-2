<p><p>The Automated Benchtop Immunoanalyzer market is divided into semi-automatic and fully-automatic types. Semi-automatic analyzers require some manual intervention for sample preparation and testing, offering a balance between efficiency and operator control. In contrast, fully-automatic analyzers perform all tasks autonomously, from sample input to result reporting, enhancing throughput and minimizing human error. Both types cater to various laboratory needs, with semi-automatic options being suitable for smaller labs, while fully-automatic systems are ideal for high-volume settings.</p></p>
