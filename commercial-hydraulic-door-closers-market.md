<p><ul><li>Surface Applied Door Closers</li><li>Concealed Door Closers</li><li>Floor Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial hydraulic door closers market includes three main types: surface applied door closers, concealed door closers, and floor springs. Surface applied door closers are mounted on the door's surface, providing visible functionality and easy installation. Concealed door closers are hidden within the door or frame, offering a sleek aesthetic while maintaining performance. Floor springs are installed in the floor, allowing for a more discreet appearance and effective operation, primarily used in heavier or more ornate doors.</p></p>
