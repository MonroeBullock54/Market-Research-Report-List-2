<p><p>Utility All Terrain Vehicles (ATVs) are categorized into two main types: crawler type and wheeled type. Crawler type ATVs feature continuous tracks, providing enhanced stability and traction on rugged terrains, making them ideal for heavy-duty tasks in challenging environments. Wheeled type ATVs, on the other hand, have rubber or pneumatic tires, offering greater mobility and speed on various surfaces, making them suitable for lighter applications and recreational use. Each type serves distinct needs based on terrain and usage requirements.</p></p>
