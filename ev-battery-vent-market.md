<p><strong>The EV Battery Vent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ePTFE Membrane Vents</li><li>Sintered PTFE Membrane Vents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EV battery vent market comprises primarily two types: ePTFE membrane vents and sintered PTFE membrane vents. ePTFE membrane vents utilize expanded polytetrafluoroethylene, offering high permeability and excellent gas release while preventing moisture ingress. Sintered PTFE membrane vents, on the other hand, are made from sintered PTFE particles, providing robust protection against particulate contamination while allowing for necessary gas exchange. Both types are crucial for maintaining battery performance and safety by managing pressure and promoting thermal stability in electric vehicles.</p></p>
