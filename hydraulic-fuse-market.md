<p><strong>The Hydraulic Fuse Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Relief Type</li><li>Reflux Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic fuse market features two main types: pressure relief and reflux. Pressure relief types protect hydraulic systems by automatically diverting fluid when pressure exceeds a preset limit, safeguarding components from damage. In contrast, reflux types allow fluid flow in one direction while blocking reverse flow, ensuring system integrity during fluctuations. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing safety and reliability in various applications, including aerospace, automotive, and industrial machinery, catering to the growing demand for safer hydraulic systems.</p></p>
