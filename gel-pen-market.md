<p><strong>The Gel Pen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capped</li><li>Retractable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gel pen market is primarily divided into two types: capped and retractable pens. Capped gel pens feature a removable cap that protects the ink tip when not in use, making them ideal for long-term storage. They often offer a variety of colors and designs. Retractable gel pens, on the other hand, have a push mechanism that allows users to extend and retract the writing tip, providing convenience and preventing ink drying. Both types cater to different user preferences and lifestyles.</p></p>
