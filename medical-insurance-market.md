<p><ul><li>Insured Liability</li><li>Payment Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical insurance market can be categorized primarily into insured liability and payment method markets. Insured liability focuses on the risk-sharing aspect, where insurers provide coverage for health-related costs incurred by policyholders, ensuring financial protection against high medical expenses. In contrast, the payment method market pertains to how healthcare providers are compensated for services rendered, including fee-for-service, bundled payments, and capitation. Each type aims to balance cost control, provider incentives, and patient access to quality healthcare.</p></p>
