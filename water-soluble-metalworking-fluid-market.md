<p><p>The water-soluble metalworking fluid market comprises several types, including synthesis metalworking fluids and semisynthetic metalworking fluids. Synthesis metalworking fluids are fully formulated from synthetic components, offering excellent cooling and lubrication properties, often preferred for high-performance machining. Semisynthetic metalworking fluids, on the other hand, blend both synthetic and mineral oils, providing a compromise between performance and cost-effectiveness. Both types enhance machining processes, extending tool life and improving surface finish while being easier to handle and maintain compared to traditional oils.</p></p>
