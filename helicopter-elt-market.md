<p><p>The Helicopter Emergency Locator Transmitter (ELT) market is divided into fixed and portable segments. Fixed ELTs are permanently installed within the helicopter and are designed to activate automatically upon impact, ensuring immediate location tracking. They are typically favored for their reliability and integration with the aircraft's systems. In contrast, portable ELTs are handheld devices that can be activated manually or automatically and are often used by pilots for their versatility, allowing for quick deployment in case of emergencies outside the aircraft.</p></p>
