<p><p>The Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor market is primarily segmented into two types: Vane Meter Sensor and Hot Wire Sensor. Vane Meter Sensors utilize a mechanical vane to measure air flow, providing reliable readings suited for larger engines. In contrast, Hot Wire Sensors employ a heated thin wire element that changes resistance as air flows over it, offering high precision and rapid response times, making them ideal for modern engines. Both types are essential for optimizing fuel efficiency and engine performance.</p></p>
