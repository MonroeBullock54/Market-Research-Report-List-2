<p><ul><li>High Performance Liquid Chromatography</li><li>Simulated Moving Bed (SMB) Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molasses chromatography encompasses various techniques for separating and purifying compounds from molasses, primarily focusing on High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and Simulated Moving Bed (SMB) chromatography. HPLC leverages high pressure to enhance the separation efficiency of components, suitable for complex mixtures. In contrast, SMB chromatography employs a continuous flow system that mimics moving beds, allowing for effective separation of sugars and byproducts, making it ideal for large-scale applications in sugar industries. Both methods optimize extraction processes and improve product purity.</p></p>
