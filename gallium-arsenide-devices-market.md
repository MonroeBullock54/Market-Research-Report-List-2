<p><p>Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) devices are categorized primarily into two types: Single Junction (SI) GaAs and Solar Cell (SC) GaAs. SI GaAs refers to pristine, high-purity material used in high-frequency and optoelectronic applications, notably in high-efficiency transistors and laser diodes. On the other hand, SC GaAs pertains to devices optimized for photovoltaic applications, leveraging multiple junctions to enhance solar energy conversion efficiency. Both types serve critical roles in telecommunications, consumer electronics, and renewable energy sectors, driving innovation and performance advancements.</p></p>
