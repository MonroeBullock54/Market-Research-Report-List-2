<p><p>The ultra miniature micro switch market is categorized primarily into two types: Single Pole Double Throw (SPDT) and Double Pole Double Throw (DPDT) switches. SPDT switches control one circuit with two positions, allowing for versatile applications in compact devices. Conversely, DPDT switches manage two separate circuits simultaneously with two positions, enabling more complex configurations. Both types are crucial in applications requiring precise control in limited spaces, making them essential components in various electronic and automation systems.</p></p>
