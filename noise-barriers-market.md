<p><ul><li>Reflective type</li><li>Absorptive type</li><li>Mixed type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The noise barriers market is categorized into three main types: reflective, absorptive, and mixed. Reflective barriers deflect sound waves away from sensitive areas, making them effective in reducing noise along highways. Absorptive barriers, on the other hand, absorb sound waves, minimizing echo and reverberation, and are ideal for urban environments. Mixed barriers combine both reflective and absorptive materials, offering versatile solutions for varying noise challenges, ensuring effective noise control in diverse applications from roads to railways.</p></p>
