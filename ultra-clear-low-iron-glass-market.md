<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra-Clear Low Iron Glass market primarily consists of two types: rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, resulting in sheets with consistent thickness, often used in architectural applications. Float glass, on the other hand, is created by floating molten glass on molten tin, leading to superior flatness and clarity, making it ideal for high-visibility applications like windows and facades. Both types are valued for their enhanced transparency and minimal color distortion.</p></p>
